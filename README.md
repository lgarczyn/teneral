# Teneral

Something is wrong with the crew. It started small — a hesitation, a glance that lasted a beat too long, someone who keeps ending up alone with people who later seem different. No one knows who to trust. Everyone has a theory. Most of them are wrong.

A real-time social deduction game for 6–20 players. Players move freely between rooms, meet in secret, and lie to each other's faces.

---

## Setup

1. Shuffle and deal one role card face-down to each player.
2. Designate several rooms in the play space. Aim for roughly half as many rooms as players.
3. Start the game.

---

## Core Loop

Players move freely between rooms. **A room holds at most 3 people.**

**1v1** — Either player may initiate a card reveal. Both act on the result immediately and in secret. Cards are only ever shown in a 1v1.

**3-person room** — No card reveals. Players talk, share information, and can attempt a 2v1 kill.

**4 or more in a room** — No one may speak. Move on.

---

## Infection

Infected players switch to the alien team but do not announce it. They know who infected them, but not who any other aliens are. Only original aliens can infect — infected players cannot.

---

## Roles

**Human** — No special ability. Use words, alliances, and votes.

**Alien** — In a 1v1, show this card to infect a human. They join your team. You beat the doctor in a 1v1 — they become infected. You do not know who the other aliens are at the start.

**Doctor** — In a 1v1, show this card to heal an infected player. You cannot heal yourself. If an alien shows their card first, you become infected instead.

**Duelist** — Once per game, in a 1v1, show this card to kill the other player outright.

**Immune** — You cannot be infected. When an alien tries, nothing happens — but you must act as though it did. The alien does not know it failed.

**Empath** — In a 1v1, you may force the other player to reveal their role card. They cannot refuse. Works even when infected.

**Predisposed** — Plays as a normal human until infected. Once infected, cannot be healed. The doctor does not know the cure failed. Must lie about the source of their infection once, to the doctor who tries to heal them.

---

## 2v1 Kill

In a 3-person room, two players can kill the third by both pointing finger guns simultaneously. No declaration needed. The target is eliminated immediately.

---

## Voting

Any player can call a vote at any time. All living players vote simultaneously. Plurality eliminates. Ties result in no elimination.

---

## Win Conditions

- **Humans win** when all alien-team players are eliminated.
- **Aliens win** when all surviving players are on the alien team.
- **Tie** when no remaining player can take any action that could change the outcome.

---

## Dead Players

Dead players may observe but cannot communicate.

---

## Recommended Composition

| Players | Aliens | Doctors | Duelist | Immune | Empath | Predisposed | Humans |
|---------|--------|---------|---------|--------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 8       | 2      | 2       | 1       | 1      | 1      | 0           | 1      |
| 10      | 2      | 3       | 1       | 1      | 1      | 0           | 2      |
| 14      | 3      | 4       | 1       | 1      | 1      | 1           | 3      |

Scale aliens and doctors first. Predisposed works best in larger games.
