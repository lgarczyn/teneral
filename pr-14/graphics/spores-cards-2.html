Role Cards · v0.1
Human
No special ability.

Your only weapons are words and allies.
Doctor
In a 1v1, show this card to heal an infected player.

You cannot heal yourself. If infected, you switch sides.
Alien
In a 1v1, show this card to infect a human.

They join your team until healed. You win 1v1 against the doctor.
Immune
You cannot be infected. Your card will flip, but you stay human.

The alien does not know you are immune. You are not obliged to reveal your role when infected.
Duelist
Once per game, you can kill in a 1v1 — no ally needed.

Like everyone, you can also 2v1 kill with an ally.
Empath
In a 1v1, you may demand another player reveal their role card.

They cannot refuse. Works even when infected.
Predisposed
You play as a normal human — until infected.

Once infected, you cannot be healed. The doctor does not know the cure failed. Lie about the source of your infection. You are not obliged to reveal your role.
